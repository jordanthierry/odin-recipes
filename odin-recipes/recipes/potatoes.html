Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 4lbs of potoatoes
  • 1 1/4 cup of sour cream
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 10 tbsp of butter, melted
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Fill pot with water and potatoes and bring to boil. Reduce heat and let simmer for 25-30 minutes.
  2. Drain potatoes and let cool. Whisk together sour cream, garlic, cream, egg yolks, and butter
  3. Preheat oven to 425. Mash potatoes and add whisked mixture until desired consistency. Salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Transfer to baking dish and bake for 25-30 minutes.
