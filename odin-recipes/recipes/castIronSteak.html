Cast Iron Steak

Ingredients

  • Steak, any cut
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Fresh rosemary
  • 3 cloves of garlic, peeled and smashed
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • avocado oil

Instructons

  1. Let steaks sit and reach room temperature, ~20 minutes.
  2. Season steaks generously while pan comes to heat.
  3. Cover oil in pan. Add steak and let sit for ~4 minutes, or until crust has formed.
  4. Flip steak and let sit for ~2 min. Add butter,garlic, and rosemary to pan. Baste and flip, repeat until steak cooked to desired temperature.
